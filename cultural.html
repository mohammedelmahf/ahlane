Our Cultural Programs

Carefully curated experiences designed to help you understand and appreciate Moroccan culture deeply.

Language Exchange

Master Arabic, French, or Berber with native speakers through structured conversation practice and cultural context learning.

  • Weekly conversation sessions
  • Cultural context lessons
  • Personalized learning plans

Traditional Workshops

Learn traditional Moroccan crafts, cooking, and arts from master artisans in authentic settings.

  • Tagine cooking classes
  • Pottery and ceramics
  • Carpet weaving

Cultural Tours

Explore Morocco's historical sites, markets, and hidden gems with knowledgeable local guides who share insider stories.

  • Medina walking tours
  • Desert experiences
  • Atlas Mountains trips

Family Integration

Experience authentic Moroccan family life through carefully matched host families and community connections.

  • Host family programs
  • Traditional celebrations
  • Community events

Music & Dance

Learn traditional Moroccan music and dance forms through interactive workshops with professional artists.

  • Gnawa music sessions
  • Traditional dance classes
  • Instrument workshops

Literature & Stories

Discover Moroccan literature, folklore, and storytelling traditions that have shaped the cultural landscape.

  • Book discussion circles
  • Storytelling workshops
  • Poetry sessions
